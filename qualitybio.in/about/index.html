About Us

We are a leading biofuel company based in India, specializing in the production of high-quality biomass pellets made from mustard waste and other agricultural residues. Sourced from the fertile Hadoti region, our biomass pellets provide a sustainable, eco-friendly alternative to fossil fuels. By converting agricultural waste into energy, we aim to reduce carbon emissions while supporting local farmers and industries, contributing to a cleaner and greener future.

Our mission is to lead the transition towards sustainable energy by becoming a global pioneer in biomass fuel production. We are committed to promoting renewable energy solutions that not only enhance energy security but also reduce environmental impact. With a focus on innovation and environmental responsibility, we strive to create value for our customers while empowering local communities.

Through our dedication to quality, our biomass pellets deliver high energy efficiency, making them ideal for a wide range of heating and industrial applications. As we continue to drive innovation in the biofuel industry, we remain focused on our vision of contributing to a cleaner environment and sustainable energy for generations to come.

The pellet/Briquette plant is situated in Hisar district, Haryana. It has 4 complete Automatic pellets cum briquette production lines with rated production capacity of 120 MT/day. The size range for Pellets/Briquette starts from 16 mm and goes upto 90 mm. Briquettes/pellets can be separately produced from Mustard husk, Groundnut shell, Parali, Sawdust and other available Biomass. The average GCV ranges between 3500 – 4000 Kcal/kg with 5 – 10 % moisture and 8 – 10% Ash content in the final pellet/briquette.

At Quality group, we are committed to the UN’s sustainable development goals and adding positive impact on the environment and communities.

We look forward to catering to your valuable Biomass pellets/Briquettes requirements for the boilers.
