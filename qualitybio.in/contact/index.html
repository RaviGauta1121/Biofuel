About Us

We are a leading biofuel company based in India, specializing in the production of high-quality biomass pellets made from mustard waste and other agricultural residues. Sourced from the fertile Hadoti region, our biomass pellets provide a sustainable, eco friendly alternative to fossil fuels. With a focus on innovation and environmental responsibility, we aim to contribute to cleaner energy solutions that reduce carbon emissions while supporting local farmers and industries. Our commitment to quality ensures that our pellets offer high energy efficiency, making them ideal for a wide range of heating and industrial applications..
